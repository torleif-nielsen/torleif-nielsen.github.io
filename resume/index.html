About

Torleif hails from Honolulu, Hawaii. He has earned a degree in Computer Science with focuses in Computer Security and Web Application development from the University of Hawaii at Manoa. He held a position at the University's Apple Service center as the lead technician and database administrator of the department's SQL store for their products. He later went on to work for the Distance Course Design & Consulting Group as one of two full-stack engineers developing a Rails (4) application to provide online creation and distribution of standardized tests to Hawaiian Immersion Schools for K-12. His greatest achievements include maintaining a perfect service score in his last 5 months of work servicing Apple products, and optimizing CSV data exports in Rails to bring a 1-2 minute turn around time down to 5-10 seconds. His non-work interests involve the outdoors, publishing tutorials online, working on open source projects, and tinkering with the latest tools on the web.
